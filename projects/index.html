Major business project - DEMO Interactive Reports

With acute understanding of business needs, Krupal conceived a comprehensive Data Analytics solution combining the latest of technology available from Microsoft PowerBI platform, enmeshing it with existing ERM and CRM solutions to build over 15 interactive Business Intelligence reports that became key for sales and company strategy formulation. The reports smartly tied disparate information to produce INSIGHTFUL and ACTIONABLE data, that directly led to improved sales, key accounts performance and marketing results.

Please INTERACT with the DEMO reports by,

Quick how to :

Reports look best when viewed on a Desktop with full-screen mode
Interact by Clicking on graphs and :

  • Using [CTRL + CLICK] on different bar graphs to further FILTER by their contexts.
  • The data has been SMARTLY tied in the background to adapt to any combination of choices to present INSIGHTFUL and actionable DATA!
Filter data by :

  • Clicking on the filter options and drop down menus found on TOP of the reports, [CTRL + Click] can be used for multi-selections.
  • Additional filtering possible by clicking directly on 'bar graphs' and using [CTRL + Click] on different graph elements to select their contexts.
Explore ALL pages by :

  • Choosing different pages by clicking on the arrows in the bar BELOW the reports with title 'Microsoft Power BI' .
  • You can view the reports in 'full-screen' by clicking on the 'expand' arrow on the right corner in the same bar.
